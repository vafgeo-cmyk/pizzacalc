Technique notes

💧

Autolyse the flour

Mix Allinson's Easy Bake yeast directly into the flour (dry — no dissolving needed). Add most of the water (hold back 50g), mix to a shaggy mass and rest 30 min. Then add the salt dissolved in the reserved water.

✋

Stretch and fold, not knead

Perform 4 sets of stretch-and-folds every 30 minutes during bulk. Wet hands prevent sticking. No aggressive kneading required.

⚖️

Weigh every dough ball

Use a digital scale to portion at exactly 280g. Consistency in ball size = consistent cook time on the Arc.

🫳

Open by hand, never a rolling pin

Press from centre outward with flat fingers, leaving a 2cm rim. Lift and let gravity stretch. A rolling pin destroys the air pockets in the cornicione.

🌡️

Cold fridge = slow flavour

The long cold ferment (18–24h) develops complex lactic acid flavour. Don't skip it — this is the difference between good and exceptional pizza.

⏱️

Out of fridge timing

6–8 hours at room temperature is crucial. Cold dough is dense and won't stretch. At 20°C aim for 7 hours; in a warm kitchen (24°C+) stick to 6.

🍕

Keep pizzas warm after cooking

For a party cook, tent pizzas loosely with foil and place over a warm (not hot) oven at 80°C. Serve within 10 minutes of cooking for best texture.
